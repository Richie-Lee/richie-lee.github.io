About Me

Hey there! I’m Richie, a 22-year-old half Chinese/Vietnamese Dutchie from Eindhoven, with a passion for Econometrics and Data Science.

I’m striving to become a full-fledged “Tech Economist”, where I can combine the power of data science with economic concepts to tackle complex dynamics of human behaviour in non-standard problems. It’s a fascinating challenge, where in my opinion, this unique intersection quantitative disciplines amplify each others strengths in a really cool way. Currently, my main technical interests lie in causal inference and experimental methods. Not only because of the interesting theory, but also because I consider it to be a key foundation in supporting bold and agile innovation.

In the last few years, I’ve also grown very fond of public speaking and content creation. I enjoy sharing my ideas and engaging with audiences both as a means to express myself and as a way to connect more deeply with others. Overall it’s something I recognise as super valuable, in both my personal and professional adventures, and it’s therefore one of the traits I strive to actively work on the most atm.

Personal development and competitiveness play a significant role in my life. I thrive on pushing my limits, embracing new opportunities, and constantly growing as an individual. It’s a journey I find immensely fulfilling and rewarding.

That’s a little glimpse into who I am! If you’re interested in collaborating, discussing ideas, or simply connecting, don’t hesitate to reach out. I’m always up for engaging conversations!
